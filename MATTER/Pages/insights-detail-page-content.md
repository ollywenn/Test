## Content Structure

### Article Content Areas
1. **Hero Section**: Article title, date, category filters
2. **Main Content**: Alternating image/text sections for long-form article content
3. **Brand Integration**: Company messaging within article flow
4. **Related Content**: "More insights" recommendations
5. **Footer Navigation**: Complete site navigation and company information

### Interactive Elements
- Category filter buttons (Industry News, Resources)
- Download CTA button
- Social media links
- Navigation menu items
- Article card links

### Visual Design Notes
- **Long-form Layout**: Designed for detailed article content with multiple sections
- **Image Integration**: Large hero image and alternating content blocks with supporting imagery
- **Brand Consistency**: Maintains Zolv color scheme and typography throughout
- **Content Hierarchy**: Clear section breaks and visual rhythm for readability
- **Professional Styling**: Travel industry focus with high-quality photography and clean typography

### Responsive Considerations
- Full-width hero sections (1400px)
- Contained content areas with proper padding (110px horizontal)
- Flexible image sizing
- Mobile-friendly navigation structure
- Scalable typography system

This layout represents a detailed blog article or insights page template with comprehensive content sections, visual storytelling elements, and clear calls-to-action integrated throughout the reading experience.
