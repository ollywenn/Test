### What we’ve learned in practice

The most reliable platforms aren’t the ones with the cleanest architecture diagrams.  
They’re the ones designed with change, exception handling and operational reality in mind.

That usually means:

- Designing for failure, not just success paths
- Making trade-offs explicit
- Avoiding “clever” solutions that no one wants to maintain
